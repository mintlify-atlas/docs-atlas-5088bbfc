## Leadership Team

OWASP Nest is led by a dedicated team committed to fostering collaboration and supporting contributors. The leadership team ensures the platform aligns with OWASP's mission, continually improving its features to serve the community better.

All OWASP Nest leaders are certified ISC² professionals, OWASP members, and adhere to the OWASP Code of Conduct.

## Leadership Principles

The OWASP Nest leadership team operates with the following principles:

- **Community-First Approach:** Every decision prioritizes the needs of the OWASP community
- **Open Collaboration:** Fostering an inclusive environment for all contributors
- **Quality Standards:** Maintaining high code quality and security standards
- **Continuous Improvement:** Regularly enhancing features based on community feedback
- **Transparency:** Open communication about project direction and decisions

## Project Governance

All OWASP Nest leaders:

- Are OWASP members in good standing
- Hold current ISC² certifications
- Follow the OWASP Code of Conduct
- Commit to supporting the contributor community
- Participate in regular project maintenance and strategic planning
